Welcome to Balochistan

Balochistan is the largest province of Pakistan by area, rich in minerals and landscapes. Quetta, the capital city, is famous for its cold weather and fruit markets. Gwadar is a modern port city with great importance in trade and CPEC. Turbat is known for its hot climate and growing infrastructure. Khuzdar is a mountainous city with natural beauty and calm life. Chaman is a border city close to Afghanistan, serving as a trade gateway. Balochistan combines deserts, mountains, and coastal beauty in one diverse region.
